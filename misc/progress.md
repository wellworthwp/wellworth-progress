   - branch push (move to §1),
   - merge (move to §2),
   - block (write to §3 within 24h),
   - decision (§4 same day),
   - risk fired (§5),
   - spec change (§6 + update the master spec).

8. **Definition of Done** for every story is master spec §0.2. No shortcuts.
